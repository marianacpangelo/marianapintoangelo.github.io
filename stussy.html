stüssy




STÜSSY is an editorial artifact that was created during the first year of the Master Degree in Graphic Design and Editorial Projects of the faculty of Fine Arts of the University of Porto, within the curricular unit of Project. Focusing on time: Future, Present Past; it’s purpose aims to represent the history and evolution of the Stüssy fashion brand, beginning with a speculation about what the brand can bring in the future and, after, it will develop since the present moment (2022), until the day the designer Shawn Stüssy was born.

The book has a hard black fabric cover; the cover informations are printed in aparchment paper strip, which is evolving the book. The page layout is divided in three horizontal parts. The text at the top of the text is related to content of the future and present of the brand; the text at the center of the page is related to the present and paast; and the text at the bottom is related to the past.
