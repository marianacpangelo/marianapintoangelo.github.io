auto pilot




AUTO PILOT is an illustrated A5 fanzine which tells a story about a swimmer called Julie who is preparing to compete. In the beginning, she is warming up before the meeting. As she jumps in the water and starts swimming, she looks to the bottom of the pool and starts seeing more and more fish and corals. Julie is confused about everything and, suddenly, she strongly and violently hits the wall of the pool with her head. This project was created within the curricular unit of Contexts and Practices of Illustration, inserted in the first year of the Master Degree in Graphic Design and Editorial Projects of the faculty of Fine Arts of the University of Porto (January 2022). It is only for recreational and academic purposes.
