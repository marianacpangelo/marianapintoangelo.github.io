aristo




ARISTO is a portuguese magazine focused mainly on architecture and promoting sustainability. It covers topics such as interior design, urban design, product design and landscape architecture. The main goal is to learn about the work of several national and international studios and architects that use and work with sustainable and environmentally friendly materials. Its periodicity is quarterly, resulting in a total of 4 magazines per year. The idea was based on a hybrid object, composed with different elements in its composition: square for technical drawing, graduated ruler and protractor scale. This project was created within the curricular unit of Art Direction, inserted in the first year of the master in Graphic Design and Editorial Projects of the faculty of Fine Arts of the University of Porto (February 2022) with Luísa Lino, Maria Carneiro and Rita Silveirinha. It is only for recreational and academic purposes.

The grid shown on it’s cover and it’s color are the only elements that change in each new edition, however, the titles presented in the cover will always be in the same spaces inside the grid. Because the magazine is quarterly, we chose to rotate the grid 90º in each issue, clockwise. Thus, in the laast edition of the year, the grid will complete a 360º turn. Regarding color, each edition will present a color on the cover that is dominant in the set of images presented the whole magazine.
